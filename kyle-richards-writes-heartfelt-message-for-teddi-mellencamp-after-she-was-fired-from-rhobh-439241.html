Kyle Richards Writes Heartfelt Message For Teddi Mellencamp After She Was Fired From RHOBH

April 2024 · 2 minute read

Kyle Richards and Teddi Mellencamp have been two peas in a pod during the recent seasons of Real Housewives of Beverly Hills. Now that Teddi has been given the boot for allegedly being too ‘boring and stale,’ Kyle is going to have to find another scheming buddy.

Kyle took to Instagram to give a proper send off to her close friend.

‘To my friend @teddimellencamp who I love & adore ..I am so grateful for #RHOBH for bringing you into my life. You are an amazing Mom, wife, sister, daughter & friend. You work so hard, always giving it your all . You are so smart, kind , thoughtful, funny & real. I look forward to witnessing what the universe has in store for you next. I know it’s going to be amazing. You deserve it all. Cheers.’

Teddi responded to the beautiful message by saying: ‘For pete’s sake, I was about to fall asleep and then got this tag and had a little tear. I love you friend and can’t wait for many more years of laughter and trouble together.’

However, fans were not touched by Kyle’s words. Instead, they laid into her due to their behavior during the latest season where they were accused of ganging up on Denise Richards over those rumors that she hooked up with Brandi Glanville.

‘This is what happens when you treat people like dirt…Who needs friends who do that .!!.Your turn next Kyle…….The shows never been the same after Dear Lisa Vanderpump left…..Shes got a good Heart.’ wrote one social media user.

‘Buh-bye! Teddi latched onto you after she latched on to Vandeepump to remain relevant. You know she could never hold a storyline on her own (character development). I always thought it was odd your house was broken into after her husband installed your alarm, let that sink in. Teddi isnt the only one with a fraudulent business,’ added another.

This follower said: ‘let’s hope you’re next kyle.’
