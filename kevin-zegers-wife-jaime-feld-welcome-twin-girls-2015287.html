Kevin Zegers, Wife Jaime Feld Welcome Twin Girls

March 2024 · 3 minute read

UPDATE: They're here — for real this time! Kevin Zegers and his wife Jaime Feld have welcomed their twin daughters, Zegers announced on Twitter on Thursday, Aug. 20.

They're here! I've never been more in love than I am with the gift my beautiful wife just gave me…Zoe Madison and Blake Everleigh Zegers.

— Kevin Zegers (@KevinZegers) August 20, 2015

—-

UPDATE: Not so fast! It turns out Kevin Zegers and his wife haven't yet welcomed their twin girls. The actor noted in a tweet that a previous photo and caption he shared on Instagram may have been confusing, and clarified that his daughters are not due for a few more weeks.

Misleading Instagram I guess. Girls not here yet. Just a doctors appointment listening to heartbeats. Couple weeks to go.

— Kevin Zegers (@KevinZegers) July 28, 2015

—-

It's double the baby joy in the Zegers household! Gracepoint actor Kevin Zegers and his wife, Jaime Feld, have welcomed twin daughters, Zegers annonounced on Instagram on Monday, July 27. 

"2 girls. In sync," the proud dad captioned a photo of an EKG machine with their vitals.

2 girls. In sync.

A photo posted by kevinzegers1984 (@kevinzegers1984) on Jul 27, 2015 at 4:27pm PDT

The girls are the first children for the Gossip Girl alum and his talent agent wife of two years.

As the actor told Us Weekly mere weeks before his wife's due date, he's getting into the groove of parenthood rather well. "The nursery's all done, I've been doing that for the past month or two," said Zegers, who has posted photos of his daughters' dressers all arranged and stocked on Instagram. "It's a good way for me to keep busy. I took a few months off of work so I can focus on baby stuff."

Though they had most of the parenthood prep out of the way before delivery, the Air Bud alum and his wife wanted to wait to meet their daughters before naming them. "We have, like, four or five names, and we'll choose when we meet them . . . see what they look like," he told Us on July 14 at a Cure Batten event.

Announcing their pregnancy in April, Zegers and Feld couldn’t wait to become parents. "We’re ready. Twins is obviously a lot, but we’re just super grateful that we were able to get pregnant," the new dad told Us. "We're excited!"

Zegers was more than happy to dote on his wife, who had a very short list of pregnancy must-haves. "My wife has been great. Once a week she wants barbecue, so we go have ribs. We have lots of olives in the house . . . ice cream occasionally. But I mean, she's so tiny," marveled Zegers. "I probably put on more weight than she has while she's been pregnant."
