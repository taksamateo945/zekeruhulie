When Does Pink: All I Know So Far Come Out? Where to Watch the Pink Documentary

March 2024 · 3 minute read

Are you ready to rock? Well, you better get ready, because the new Pink documentary is coming to Amazon Prime very soon. And though it’s a little late for Mother’s Day, Pink (aka P!nk, aka Alecia Beth Moore) is bringing her rock and roll attitude both to the stage on her “Beautiful Trauma World Tour” and to her role as a mother of three children. Hey, anyone who knows a mom knows that motherhood is hardcore!

Titled after Pink’s recent single, Pink: All I Know So Far is directed by Michael Gracey, who is best known as the director of the 2017 circus musical The Greatest Showman. The film has promised to pull back the curtain and give fans a behind-the-scenes look at Pink on her record-breaking tour, balancing life on the road with her family as a mom, a performer, a wife, and a boss.

Pink fans know that the singer/songwriter is long overdue for her very own music doc, and now that documentary is finally here.

Here’s what you need to know about where to watch the Pink documentary, including the Pink: All I Know So Far release date and the Pink: All I Know So Far release time.

Where to watch the Pink documentary, Pink: All I Know So Far:

Starting this Friday, Pink: All I Know So Far will be streaming across the globe exclusively on Amazon Prime Video, free to all Prime subscribers. It will not stream anywhere else. Pink: All I Know So Far is an Amazon Studios original film, and therefore you will only be able to watch the Pink documentary if you have a subscription to Amazon Prime. If you are not a subscriber, you might consider the Amazon Prime free trial, or get login details from a friend.

When will the Pink documentary be on Amazon Prime? What is Pink: All I Know So Far release date?

Pink: All I Know So Far will begin streaming globally on Amazon Prime on Friday, May 7, 2021.

What time will Pink: All I Know So Far be on Amazon Prime?

Amazon titles usually drop at 12 a.m. GMT, which means you will likely be able to watch Pink: All I Know So Faras early as 8 p.m ET / 5 p.m. PT on Thursday evening in North America. Occasionally, Amazon titles drop even earlier than that, so you might want to start checking around 7 p.m. ET / 4 p.m. PT. If you don’t see the movie right away, keep checking back throughout the evening.
