Kendra Wilkinson Wears New Wedding Ring Following Cheating Scandal

May 2024 · 1 minute read

Kendra Wilkinson has come a long way since the finale of Kendra on Top this past November. The former Playmate has accepted the wedding ring her husband Hank Baskett offered her on the finale of the WE tv show. As previously reported, Wilkinson flushed her original wedding ring down the toilet after she learned of his infidelity.

The mom of two, who told her hubby she would only accept the new rock if he told her the truth about his affair with a transgender model, flashed the bling while posing alongside the former NFL pro on Wednesday, March 11.

The ring, which Wilkinson called an upgrade, features a massive yellow diamond which Baskett explained stands for friendship.
